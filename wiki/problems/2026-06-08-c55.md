## ✍️ 풀이 핵심
- $\lim\frac{f+g}{x}=3$ → $f(0)+g(0)=0$, $f'(0)+g'(0)=3$
- $\lim\frac{f+3}{xg}=2$ → $f(0)=-3$(분자 0), $g(0)=3$; $\frac{f'(0)}{g(0)}=2$ → $f'(0)=6,\ g'(0)=-3$
- $h'(0)=f'(0)g(0)+f(0)g'(0)=18+9=27$
